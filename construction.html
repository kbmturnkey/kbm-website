Our Contract Models

We offer two different Home Construction Contract Models to suit the unique needs and preferences of our clients:

Lump-Sum Contract

Our Lump-Sum Contract offers a fixed price for the complete construction project, based on clearly defined specifications and scope. It ensures budget certainty, streamlined execution, and full responsibility on our part for timely and quality delivery.

NEW Cost-Plus Contract SAVE 10%

Our Cost-Plus Contract model provides complete transparency and flexibility, where clients pay the actual construction costs plus a fixed service fee. It is best suited for those who want greater control over material selection and design decisions, ensuring cost efficiency and adaptability throughout the project lifecycle.
